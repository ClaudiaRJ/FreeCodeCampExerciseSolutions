  <h1>Chocolate cookies</h1>
  <p>A recipe for chocolate cookies</p>
  <h2>Ingredients</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>100g butter (at room temperature)</li>
      <li>100g sugar (white or brown)</li>
      <li>1 egg</li>
      <li>1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)</li>
      <li>180g all-purpose flour</li>
      <li>1/2 tsp baking powder</li>
      <li>1 pinch of salt</li>
      <li>100g chocolate chips or chunks</li>
    </ul>
  <h2>Instructions</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).</li>
      <li>In a bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.</li>
      <li>Add the egg and vanilla extract and mix well.</li>
      <li>Add the flour, baking powder, and salt. Mix until just combined.</li>
      <li>Shape the dough into small balls and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, leaving space between them.</li>
      <li>Flatten them slightly with your hand or a fork.</li>
      <li>Bake for 10–12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.</li>
    </ol>
